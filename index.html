Consulting

Consulting - Challenges

IT Consulting comes in many flavors - Individual freelance consultants to Managed IT Services. However, the challenges are the same.

  1. Knowledge Gap - If you are filling a knowledge Gap, there is no way to validate if the consultant or the consulting company is the right fit for your needs.
  2. Honest effort estimation and time-keeping - The least count for a consulting engagement is normally 1 FTE or sometimes 1/2 FTE. What this means is whether the resource(s) have work or not they keep charging 40 or 20 hour per week.
  3. Effectively Manage Consulting resource - You as a client, has to keep providing work to the consulting 'machine' while also socializing the requirements and confirming the same with the rest of the stake holder community. All this while the other business functions that keep all the stake holders and PMO tethered to their day jobs.
Digital Biz Tech's Solution

Digital Biz Tech's solutions are informed by our core values - 1) Focus on providing value, honestly. 2) First principle thinking 3) Returns through compounding and long term relationship 4) KISSS.

  1. Knowledge Gap - We will identify a small object/part of the project and build/deliver it free-of-cost. Up to 8 hrs of free consulting will be provided to achieve and validate our value delivery approach.
  2. Honest effort estimation and time-keeping - We start with honest time estimations for tasks - providing constant feedback loop in case of additional scope or any changes that would increase/decrease the estimation. We also maintain detailed work-log - which includes time consumed for each task. Our experts work on multiple projects and will only charge you the time they have worked on your project.
  3. Effectively Manage Consulting resource - The tools of detailed work log and the flexibility of spot consulting - by only charging you for the time consultants have worked on your project - allows you the flexibility in managing the resources.
