Consulting

General Consulting Challenges

From individual freelance consultants to IT consulting corporations, IT consulting has many different forms; however, all face similar challenges.

  1. Knowledge Gap - When using consultants to fill in a knowledge gap, it becomes difficult for clients to validate if the consultant is the right fit for their needs.
  2. Honest effort estimation and time-keeping - The least count for a consulting engagement is normally 1 FTE or sometimes 1/2 FTE. What this means is whether the resource(s) have work or not they keep charging 40 or 20 hour per week.
  3. Effectively Manage Consulting resource - You as a client, has to keep providing work to the consulting 'machine' while also socializing the requirements and confirming the same with the rest of the stake holder community. All this while the other business functions that keep all the stake holders and PMO tethered to their day jobs.
Digital Biz Tech's Solution

Our solutions are informed by our core values - 1) Honestly providing value 2) First principles thinking 3) Compounding/long-term relationships 4) KISSS.

  1. Knowledge Gap - We will identify a small object/part of the project and develop it free of cost. Up to 8 hours of free consulting will be provided for the client to validate our value delivery approach.
  2. Honest effort estimation and time-keeping - We start with honest time estimations for tasks, providing a constant feedback loop to accommodate any changes that would increase/decrease the workload. We also maintain detailed work-log, which includes hours taken for each task. Though we work on multiple projects simultaneously, our fee depends solely on the time spent on one client’s project
  3. Effectively Manage Consulting resource - By recording a detailed work log, maintaining flexible spot consulting, and charging based on work done solely for one client, we seamlessly integrate into our client’s corporation
